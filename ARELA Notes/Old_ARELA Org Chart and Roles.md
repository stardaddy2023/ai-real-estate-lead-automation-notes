## Detailed Role Descriptions

### 1. The Executive Suite

- **Human CEO (You):**
    
    - **Role:** The vision, the bank account, and the "Kill Switch." You set the Buy Box (e.g., "Pinal County, Cash Flow > $300/mo") and approve offers generated by the AI.
        
    - **Direct Report:** The AI Orchestrator.
        
- **AI Orchestrator (The Brain):**
    
    - **Role:** The middleware (like LangChain or Zapier) that passes data between agents. It ensures `Scout` passes the zip codes to `Bloodhound` without data loss.
        

### 2. Acquisitions Department ("The Hunter")

- **Agent: SCOUT (Market Intelligence)**
    
    - **Function:** Monitors macro-data.
        
    - **Task:** "Alert me when inventory in Maricopa rises by 10%."
        
- **Agent: BLOODHOUND (Data Engineer)**
    
    - **Function:** Scrapes county records, filters for distress (Divorce, Tax Liens), and integrates with Skip Trace APIs.
        
    - **Task:** "Find phone numbers for all absentee owners in 85713."
        
- **Agent: SIREN (Inside Sales/Outreach)**
    
    - **Function:** Handles the "First Touch." Sends SMS drips and Ringless Voicemails. Uses NLP to categorize responses (Interested vs. "Stop").
        
    - **Task:** "Engage 500 leads. If they reply 'Yes', move to Analysis."
        

### 3. Analysis Department ("The Architect")

- **Agent: SHERLOCK (Valuations)**
    
    - **Function:** pulls comps from MLS/APIs. Adjusts for square footage, year built, and lot size.
        
    - **Task:** "Determine ARV for 2932 W Enchanted Hills."
        
- **Agent: REHAB-ESTIMATOR (Vision)**
    
    - **Function:** Uses Computer Vision to analyze listing photos. Identifies "dated kitchen," "damaged roof," or "missing flooring" and assigns cost estimates.
        
- **Agent: CFO-BOT (Underwriting)**
    
    - **Function:** Runs the **KPI Sweep**. Calculates NOI, Cap Rate, and MAO (Max Allowable Offer).
        
    - **Task:** "Does this deal meet the 1% rule? If not, structure a Seller Finance offer."
        

### 4. Transactions Department ("The Closer")

- **Agent: SCRIBE (Legal/Docs)**
    
    - **Function:** Generates LOIs (Letters of Intent) and Purchase Agreements.
        
    - **Task:** "Draft a Subject-To contract with a $10k entry fee."
        
- **Agent: GUARDIAN (Transaction Coordinator)**
    
    - **Function:** Manages deadlines. Reminds you to deposit EMD (Earnest Money). Emails the Title Company.
        
    - **Task:** "Schedule inspection for Tuesday."
        

### 5. Dispositions Department ("The Cash Register")

- **Agent: HYPE-MAN (Marketing)**
    
    - **Function:** Writes listing descriptions ("Investor Special!"). Selects the best photos.
        
- **Agent: MATCHMAKER (Sales)**
    
    - **Function:** Manages your Cash Buyers list. Tags buyers by their criteria (e.g., "Mike buys in Tucson"). Matches your new deal to the right buyers and sends the email blast.
